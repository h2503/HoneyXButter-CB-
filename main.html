The Story of Honey X Butter

We are two friends who wanted to start a small 'Sambal' (chili paste) manufacturing business. While operating it homebased in 2018, we started to gain some loyal following. Our philosophy has always been simple, we care about creating delicious food that makes you, our familia, feel good. From 'Day-1', your feedbacks has alway been 'The King' to our growth. In fact, the existence of Honey X Butter was simply because of you all, whom requested for us to create a chicken dish...

Adjusting to The Local Taste

We crafted a double-fry battered chicken to produce that right amount of crunch using our secret dry-mixture. So then we glazed it with our signature sauce to create that sweet yet savoury taste,that makes you keep wanting to come again. In general, Yes! asians like spicy food. Hence we created our Extra-Spicy Honey Butter sauce that alwasy caught our daring customers off-guard.

Our Short Cameo at Jerudong Park Foodcourt

in November 2020, We were lucky to be invited by jerudong Park Management to operate at their foodcourt. Which quickly, we took it as part of our pilot project. That short 3 months enable us to tweak our recipes to our customers liking, learn about running a food & beverages business and most importantly, to getting to know you all.

Keeping that 'Halal' gap

Our chicken is 100% Bruneian and proudly certified 'Halal' by MORA. We also take a serious attention to our food preparation method and hygiene, for your health and safety.

Community

We treat our customers like family. We try hard to remember every faces of our returning customers, and to prepare their foods according to each of their unique preferences.
